GOVTECH FORUM 2020

GovTech is a whole-of-government approach to public sector modernization that promotes

simple, accessible, and efficient government. It aims to promote the use of technology to

transform the public sector, improve service delivery to citizens and businesses

and increase efficiency, transparency and accountability.
